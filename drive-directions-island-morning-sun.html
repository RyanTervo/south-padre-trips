Driving Directions

  1. Directions as you cross the Causeway/Bridge. Take a LEFT onto Padre Blvd highway 100. You will proceed approximately 2.9 miles, or 7 minutes north to 103 W. Morningside Drive. Highway 100, or Padre Blvd is the main drag on the island, the 4 lane highway. You will eventually see Island Market on your right at the 2.5 miles mark. About 3 blocks north, you will see Kelly's Irish Pub on your right and that is Morning Side Drive so take a LEFT at the traffic signal. You will see an empty lot to the left (west) of Padre Blvd on Morningside dr.

  2. The first quad plex/4 plex on the LEFT a pale yellow/beige building is Island Morning Sun Condos. It does not have a marker sign. The Parking is reserved (dedicated) for each condo unit. There is space for 2 cars if you park front to back, not side by side. Unit ONE is all the way to the right.

  • Unit TWO parking is the 2nd from the RIGHT. Unit THREE is the 2nd spot from the LEFT. Do NOT park on the median / side of the street as Police can order a tow and it is over $300 to pay to get it out of impound.

  • The HOA does not allow for more than 2 vehicles. Unit ONE/A is the first door ground floor on the left side of building. Entry is by door code, no key. If you have ANY troubles, call our office 956.242.0904 there is an option for after hours assistance.
